Description

After recovering his son from the hands of Cipher, Dom Toretto leads a quiet life, far from the asphalt, with Letty and their son, little Brian. But they both know that behind the most radiant horizons always hide the sneakiest dangers. This time, to save those he cares about the most, Dom will have to face the demons of his past. His team gathers to dismantle a global conspiracy led by the most relentless killer they've ever faced, as deadly with a gun as he is behind the wheel: A man who is none other than Dom's disavowed brother "Jakob".
